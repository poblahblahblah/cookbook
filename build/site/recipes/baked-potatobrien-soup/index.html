Baked PotatO'Brien Soup

November 2017 on potato, soup

A staple in the SF O'Brien home.

Ingredients:

  • ~3 lbs Russet Potatoes, baked (stab with fork generously)
  • ~1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • ~3-4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • ~¼ cup butter
  • ~1 quart chicken broth
  • ~3 teaspoon salt
  • ~2 teaspoon pepper
  • ~1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ~1 cup cream or half and half
  • ~a handful of chopped green onions
  • chopped bacon

Instructions:

  1. Bake the potatoes for 90 minutes at 300F.
  2. Combine potatoes, chopped onion, chopped garlic, butter, chicken broth, salt, and pepper in a slow cooker on low for 8 hours.
  3. Mash the potatoes to desired consistency. I like lightly chunky.
  4. Once finished, add in cheddar cheese until melted.
  5. Add in cream or half and half and green onions.
  6. Top with chopped bacon and serve.
