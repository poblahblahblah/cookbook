Pancakes

April 2023 on breakfast

We’ve been going through a pancake phase lately and these are quite good.

Makes ~8 very large pancakes.

Ingredients:

Dry Mix:

  • 385g All Purpose Flour
  • 50g sugar
  • 10g salt
  • 11g Baking Powder
  • 5g Baking Soda

Wet Mix:

  • 460ml/g Milk
  • 50ml/g Vegetable Oil
  • 9g Vanilla Extract
  • 55g Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 3 Whole Eggs

Additional Ingredients:

  • Salted Butter
  • Maple Syrup
  • Peanut Butter? Whipped Cream? Go wild.

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl combine the dry ingredients together and thoroughly mix to combine.

  2. In a separate bowl combine the wet ingredients together and thoroughly mix to combine.

  3. Pour the contents of the wet bowl into the dry bowl. Mix to combine, no more than 20-25 stirs. The batter should be thick and chunky.

  4. Preheat your pan or griddle over medium temperature and add a generous pad of butter.

  5. Once the butter is melted, scoop a generous amount of the batter onto the griddle. This should be pretty thick, maybe ½ inch or so thick.

  6. Cook until crispy. Flip and cook the other side until crispy.
